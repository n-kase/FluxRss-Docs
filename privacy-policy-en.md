# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FluxRss (the “App”) handles user data. The App respects user privacy and is designed to operate without collecting personal information.

---

## 1. Information Collected
The App does not collect any personal data.

The following information may be stored locally on the user’s device:

- RSS feed data  
- Read/starred article states  
- App settings (language, UI, AI, Read Later integrations, etc.)  
- Authentication credentials for external services (FreshRSS, Readwise, Instapaper)

All data is stored **locally on the device or in iCloud Key-Value Store** and is **never transmitted to external servers** by the App.

---

## 2. External Service Integrations
The App may integrate with the following external services:

- FreshRSS (Google Reader API compatible)
- Readwise Reader
- Instapaper
- OpenAI / Google Gemini (AI summary features)

API keys and authentication credentials entered by the user are securely stored in the device Keychain.  
The App does not collect or transmit these credentials.

---

## 3. Data Sync
The App uses iCloud Key-Value Store to synchronize settings and read/starred states across devices.  
All synced data is encrypted through Apple’s iCloud infrastructure.

---

## 4. Analytics and Tracking
The App does not use analytics tools, tracking technologies, or advertising frameworks.

---

## 5. Contact
For privacy-related inquiries, please contact the developer via the App Store support link.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will take effect when posted on this page.
